## Foundations & References

- [IUPAC](https://iupac.org/) – International authority on chemical nomenclature, terminology, and standards.
- [NIST Chemistry WebBook](https://webbook.nist.gov/chemistry/) – Thermochemical, spectral, and physical property data.
- [Royal Society of Chemistry](https://www.rsc.org/) – Publications, databases, and professional resources.
- [ACS Publications](https://pubs.acs.org/) – Peer-reviewed journals across all chemistry disciplines.
- [Chemistry Stack Exchange](https://chemistry.stackexchange.com/) – Community-driven Q&A for chemistry problems and theory.

## Chemical Databases & Open Data

- [PubChem](https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) – Open database of chemical structures, properties, and bioactivity.
- [ChemSpider](https://www.chemspider.com/) – Structure-centric chemical database curated by the RSC.
- [ChEMBL](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/) – Bioactive molecule database with drug-like properties.
- [Protein Data Bank (PDB)](https://www.rcsb.org/) – 3D structures of proteins, nucleic acids, and complexes.
- [ZINC Database](https://zinc.docking.org/) – Free database of commercially available compounds for virtual screening.
- [Open Reaction Database](https://open-reaction-database.org/) – Open repository of chemical reaction data.

## Computational Chemistry

- [Gaussian](https://gaussian.com/) – Widely used software for electronic structure calculations.
- [ORCA](https://orcaforum.kofo.mpg.de/) – Free quantum chemistry package for DFT and ab initio methods.
- [NWChem](https://www.nwchem-sw.org/) – Open-source computational chemistry package for large-scale systems.
- [GROMACS](https://www.gromacs.org/) – Molecular dynamics software optimized for biomolecules.
- [LAMMPS](https://www.lammps.org/) – Molecular dynamics simulator for classical and materials chemistry.
- [CP2K](https://www.cp2k.org/) – Simulation package for atomistic simulations of solid-state and molecular systems.

## Cheminformatics & Molecular Modeling

- [RDKit](https://www.rdkit.org/) – Open-source toolkit for cheminformatics and molecular modeling.
- [Open Babel](https://openbabel.org/) – Chemical toolbox for file conversion and structure manipulation.
- [CDK (Chemistry Development Kit)](https://cdk.github.io/) – Java library for structural cheminformatics.
- [DeepChem](https://deepchem.io/) – Machine learning toolkit for chemistry and drug discovery.
- [KNIME Analytics Platform](https://www.knime.com/) – Visual workflow tool with strong cheminformatics extensions.

## Quantum Chemistry

- [Psi4](https://psicode.org/) – Open-source quantum chemistry program for ab initio calculations.
- [Q-Chem](https://www.q-chem.com/) – Commercial electronic structure software suite.
- [PySCF](https://pyscf.org/) – Python-based quantum chemistry framework.
- [Molpro](https://www.molpro.net/) – High-accuracy wavefunction-based quantum chemistry software.
- [Dalton](https://daltonprogram.org/) – Program for molecular electronic structure and properties.

## Materials & Solid-State Chemistry

- [Materials Project](https://materialsproject.org/) – Open database of computed material properties.
- [AFLOW](https://aflow.org/) – Framework for high-throughput materials discovery.
- [VASP](https://www.vasp.at/) – First-principles simulation package for materials modeling.
- [Quantum ESPRESSO](https://www.quantum-espresso.org/) – Integrated suite for electronic-structure calculations.
- [ASE (Atomic Simulation Environment)](https://wiki.fysik.dtu.dk/ase/) – Python library for atomistic simulations.

## Organic Chemistry

- [Organic Reactions Wiki](https://organicchemistrydata.org/) – Curated database of organic reactions.
- [Name Reactions](https://www.organic-chemistry.org/namedreactions/) – Reference for named organic chemistry reactions.
- [Reaxys](https://www.elsevier.com/solutions/reaxys) – Comprehensive database for chemical reactions and synthesis.
- [SciFinder](https://www.cas.org/solutions/scifinder) – Research discovery tool for chemical literature.
- [RXN for Chemistry](https://rxn.res.ibm.com/) – AI-powered reaction prediction and synthesis planning.

## Inorganic & Coordination Chemistry

- [Cambridge Structural Database (CSD)](https://www.ccdc.cam.ac.uk/) – Repository of small-molecule crystal structures.
- [ICSD](https://icsd.products.fiz-karlsruhe.de/) – Inorganic crystal structure database.
- [Crystallography Open Database](https://www.crystallography.net/cod/) – Open-access crystal structure database.
- [VESTA](https://jp-minerals.org/vesta/en/) – Visualization software for crystal structures and volumetric data.

## Analytical Chemistry

- [MassBank](https://massbank.eu/MassBank/) – Open mass spectral database.
- [NMRShiftDB](https://nmrshiftdb.nmr.uni-koeln.de/) – Open database for NMR chemical shifts.
- [OpenChrom](https://www.openchrom.net/) – Open-source software for chromatography and mass spectrometry.
- [XCMS](https://xcmsonline.scripps.edu/) – Software for processing and analyzing LC/MS data.
- [Skyline](https://skyline.ms/project/home/software/Skyline/begin.view) – Targeted proteomics and quantitative analysis tool.

## Laboratory Tools & Automation

- [OpenTrons](https://opentrons.com/) – Open-source liquid-handling robots for lab automation.
- [Pythia ChemOS](https://chemos.ai/) – AI-driven platform for autonomous chemistry experiments.
- [ChemOS](https://chemos.ai/) – Orchestration system for self-driving laboratories.
- [Labguru](https://www.labguru.com/) – Electronic lab notebook (ELN) and lab management system.
- [Benchling](https://www.benchling.com/) – Cloud-based ELN and collaboration platform for life sciences.

## Visualization & Plotting

- [Avogadro](https://avogadro.cc/) – Open-source molecular editor and visualization tool.
- [PyMOL](https://pymol.org/) – Molecular visualization system for 3D structures.
- [Jmol](http://jmol.sourceforge.net/) – Java-based viewer for chemical structures.
- [VMD](https://www.ks.uiuc.edu/Research/vmd/) – Visualization tool for molecular dynamics simulations.
- [Matplotlib](https://matplotlib.org/) – Python plotting library commonly used in chemistry research.

## Learning Resources

### Tutorials
- [Chem LibreTexts](https://chem.libretexts.org/) – Open-access chemistry textbooks and learning modules.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare – Chemistry](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/chemistry/) – University-level chemistry courses.
- [Khan Academy Chemistry](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/chemistry) – Beginner-friendly chemistry lessons.

### Guides
- [Organic Chemistry Portal](https://www.organic-chemistry.org/) – Reference materials and reaction guides.
- [NMR Guide](https://www.spectroscopyonline.com/) – Practical guides for NMR and spectroscopy techniques.
- [Computational Chemistry Wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computational_chemistry) – Overview of methods and tools.

### Courses
- *Physical Chemistry* – Thermodynamics, kinetics, and quantum chemistry foundations.
- *Organic Chemistry* – Reaction mechanisms and synthesis strategies.
- *Computational Chemistry* – Simulation and modeling of molecular systems.
